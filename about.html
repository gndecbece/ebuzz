Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College, Bidar, a premier center of technical Education, was established in August, 1980 by the Prabandhak Committee, Gurudwara Sri Nanak Jhira Saheb, Bidar as per the Govt. of Karnataka Order No. ED 150 TEC 79, dated 8th August, 1980. College is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and Accredited by National Board of Accreditation (N.B.A.), New Delhi during the year 2005 and 2009. According to Washington accord in the year 2022 college has once again accredited with four departments ( ECE, CSE, EEE, CIVIL ).Our college accreditation includes recognition from both the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) and the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with A+ grade.

The department of Electronics and Communication Engineering has been established in the year of 1982.The department boasts of a team of highly qualified, dedicated and experienced faculty. In the department, 42% of faculty are doctorates and 21% are pursuing Ph.D. from reputed Institutes across the country. E&CE offers a great ambience for the wholesome development of students. With an ever growing call quality education and research, the department is committed to excellence in education. It has stringent recruitment policies, high standards for academic performance, and outstanding infrastructure.

Silicon association (SiA), an E&CE department student chapter was established in 2009. The organization has emerged as a platform overseeing the interest of the students. SiA is conceived with an idea to provide a platform for its members to organize events, social awareness and beneficial camp, lectures through the year. Active participation equips and empowers students with experience, confidence and personal growth when they come out from their academic precincts. SiA aims to showcase the hidden talent of every student by providing platform in technical and non - technical fields.
